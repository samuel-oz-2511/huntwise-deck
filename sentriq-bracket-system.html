06 — Why This Works
Same mark. Two meanings.
Cloudflare built brand recognition by making their logo appear at the exact moment of verification. Sentriq can do the same — the bracket is the gate, the V is the clearance. One mark. No secondary verification icon to design, explain, or maintain.
Rectangle geometry.
The bracket is four rectangles. No curves, no arcs, no SVG complexity. The arms map directly to UI components: dividers, frames, progress indicators, selection states. The brand geometry IS the design system. No gap between icon and interface.
The V is unmistakable.
At 16px on a candidate card, recruiters see a small amber square with a white checkmark. They don't need to know it's a V. They don't need to know it's Sentriq. They just know: cleared. Brand education happens automatically through repeated exposure to that confirmation moment.
