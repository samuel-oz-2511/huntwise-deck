The Threat

The pre-hire surface is the least defended entry point in the enterprise.

Every other attack surface has a detection layer. The hiring pipeline does not. A candidate who clears HR reaches systems, secrets, and people — before any endpoint, identity, or access control sees them.

State-sponsored operatives
320+ DPRK operative cases in 2024 — up 220% YoY. Stolen SSNs, fabricated GitHub histories, AI coaching during live video interviews. KnowBe4 hired one. He passed four video interviews and a full background check.
Deepfake and AI-coached interviews
Live AI overlay tools allow a proxy to answer technical questions in real time. The candidate in the video is not the employee who shows up. Behavioral detection at application stage catches the fingerprint mismatch before the interview is scheduled.
Coordinated bot campaigns
YC-funded Tsenta: 1,500 applications per day per user. Same behavioral fingerprint across dozens of companies simultaneously. No single employer sees the pattern. A cross-company graph does.
Ghost employment / simultaneous jobs
Employees holding 4-5 simultaneous roles — primarily a US problem due to the weakest pre-hire identity controls in any developed market. Three-day window post-hire before any identity check is legally required.

The Architecture — Shift Left

Catch threats at the perimeter.
Not after they are inside.

Sentriq is the pre-authentication trust layer for hiring. It generates a trust signal before a candidate enters the ATS, before a recruiter spends time, before an interview is scheduled. Zero automated rejections. Full audit trail. Human-in-the-loop by architecture — not by disclaimer.

Layer 1 — Passive
Behavioral Detection
Keystroke dynamics, mouse patterns, form-fill timing. Invisible to the candidate. Detects bots, synthetic applications, behavioral mismatch, and mass-apply fingerprints at submission. No friction. No candidate awareness.
→
Layer 2 — Async Challenge
Skills Verification
Only triggered when Layer 1 surfaces anomalies. Candidates who clear the passive layer are never challenged. A short task, framed as part of the application. A proxy cannot replicate both the behavioral fingerprint and the demonstrated knowledge of the person they are impersonating.
→
Layer 3 — Human Review
Analyst + Employer Decision
Sentriq analyst reviews the full signal stack. Employer makes the final call. Zero automated rejections. Full audit trail for EU AI Act compliance and legal defense.
→
Output
Trust Signal
Travels with the application into the ATS, through recruiter review, and into the hiring decision. Every downstream system benefits from the pre-authentication verdict.

The Candidate Hiring Captcha. Not a filter. Not a replacement for the ATS. The trust layer the hiring pipeline never had.

Moat 1 — The Adversarial Corpus

Huntwise: a live tool that helped candidates beat ATS systems. 100 beta users. $300K founders' capital. 30+ live scoring experiments on production ATS systems. 17 rules ATS companies did not know they were enforcing. Every attack method became a detection signature. No competitor approached this problem from the inside. You cannot buy this knowledge. You have to earn it by running the attacks.

Moat 2 — The Cross-Company Knowledge Graph
A threat intelligence network for hiring fraud.
The same bot fingerprint at Company A is flagged automatically at Company B. The same state-sponsored credential cluster at three employers triggers network-level detection at the fourth. At 15 customers, cross-company pattern matching begins. At 100, coordinated campaigns are detected before they reach any single employer.

Why nobody else can build this: Greenhouse's customer contracts legally prohibit cross-employer data sharing. Every employer-deployed product faces the same constraint. Sentriq operates as a neutral intermediary with direct candidate consent — the only architecture that makes a cross-employer fraud graph legally and technically possible. This is a permanent structural barrier, not a feature gap.
