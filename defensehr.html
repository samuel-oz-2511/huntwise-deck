Unfair Advantage

What we know that nobody else does.

9 months of empirical ATS research. 30+ controlled tests. Every finding is a detection rule.

AI-Based ATS Scoring Model

We discovered that AI-based ATS (like Comeet) assigns a fixed total penalty based on overall fit, then redistributes it. This means keyword injection doesn't reduce penalties — it just moves them around. No competitor knows this.

Overclaim Detection Signatures

Overclaiming skills costs -6 to -8 penalty vs -3 to -5 for genuine gaps. We can detect when candidates add skills they don't have evidence for — a pattern invisible to generic AI content detectors.

Hidden Keyword Injection

Candidates hide white-text keywords in resumes. We know the exact patterns because we built the optimizers. We discovered that comprehensive hidden keyword lists actually trigger penalties on AI-based ATS — a finding no one else has.

Technical vs Non-Technical Split

We proved that technical roles benefit from AI optimization but non-technical roles are hurt by it. This bifurcation means our detection engine can tune sensitivity by role type — reducing false positives dramatically.
