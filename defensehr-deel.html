The Product

The Candidate Application Firewall. CAF.

Candidate
Application
→
Sentriq CAF
Trust Signal
→
Recruiter
Decides

ATS-agnostic. Works with any system: Greenhouse, Comeet, Workday, paper, or no ATS at all.

Layer 1: Auto-Flag

Incoming applications analyzed for integrity signals: credential fabrication, timeline manipulation, mass-apply patterns, behavioral anomalies. Suspicious applications routed to review. Nothing silently removed. Ever.

Layer 2: Async Challenge

A 2-minute task sent to flagged candidates. Captures keystroke dynamics, response timing, copy-paste behavior, and revision patterns. Surfaces what a live interview would miss. Results feed back into the signal layer.

Layer 3: Human Review

Recruiter sees evidence cards, not scores. A human always decides. Zero automated rejections, ever. EU AI Act-compliant by design: every decision logged, timestamped, and traceable.

Not an ATS. Not an AI detector. Not a background check. ATS gives fit score. Sentriq gives trust signal.

Defensibility

Four structural advantages. Each compounds with usage.

01

Behavioral biometrics baseline

The async challenge captures keystroke dynamics, time-to-first-response, and revision behavior. After thousands of sessions, we build baseline distributions of what genuine expertise looks like under pressure vs. AI-assisted responses. This calibration is time-gated. A competitor starting today starts with zero data.

02

Cross-company graph — legally unavailable to ATS

ATS platforms contractually cannot share candidate data across employers. Sentriq sits between the candidate and the human, getting direct candidate consent as an independent layer. That consent mechanism is structurally unavailable to any ATS, ever. At 15 companies, our graph detects fraud no single company sees alone.

03

Regulatory audit trail as switching cost

Every hiring decision through Sentriq is logged, timestamped, and tied to a human review action. For financial services, healthcare, and defense contractors, that audit history has legal value under EU AI Act. Switching means starting the compliance record from zero.

04

Proprietary ground-truth dataset

Two years of Huntwise experiments produced the only known dataset of how hiring signal breaks from the inside: which fabrications pass which ATS, which patterns fool which scoring engines. No academic corpus, no synthetic data. Real manipulation, real platform responses.
