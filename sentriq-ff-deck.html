The Problem

Hiring fraud is real and getting much worse.

You have seen the headlines: North Korean operatives applying to US tech and defense companies, getting hired, and stealing intellectual property. This is not a fringe event. The FBI and Department of Justice have issued formal advisories. Multiple indictments have named US companies that were successfully infiltrated.

Beyond nation-state threats, AI has made it possible for anyone to generate a convincing CV, pass an automated screening round, and apply to 1,500 jobs per day on autopilot. One Deel operations role in 2024 received 6,500 applications in two weeks. Recruiters estimated 90% were noise or fraud.

The hiring funnel is the last major attack surface without a dedicated security layer. Email has spam filters. Web traffic has firewalls. Identity has verification. The job application has nothing. That is the gap Sentriq fills.

The Product

A trust layer for job applications.

Think of it as a CAPTCHA for hiring. When you submit a web form and see "I'm not a robot," that is Cloudflare's bot detection running silently before you ever reach the site. Sentriq is the same concept applied to job applications: a pre-submission layer that analyzes behavioral signals and surfaces a trust signal to the recruiter, before the application enters the ATS.

Recruiters do not see a score or a decision. They see one signal per application: review this one. No automated rejections. No biometrics. A human makes the call.

1
Behavioral Baseline
Runs silently on every application. Flags statistical anomalies: typing cadence, paste patterns, form completion timing, cross-platform consistency. No friction for real candidates.
2
Trust Layer
Triggered for flagged applications only (5-10%). A short behavioral challenge calibrated to the specific role and claimed background. Automation tools cannot pass it. Real candidates barely notice it.
3
Async Personalized Challenge
Human analyst review for escalated applications. One trust signal surfaced in the recruiter's review queue. EU AI Act-compliant human oversight built in.
