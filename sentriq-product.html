Async Challenge

Content is secondary. Sentriq scores behavioral biometrics during the response: typing consistency with Layer 1 baseline, hesitation, correction rate, response timing. A proxy operating on behalf of someone else cannot replicate the fingerprint of the person they impersonate.

CAF — how it processes a candidate
Layer 1 — Auto-flag
Passive baseline captured at submission. Score calculated in real time. Clean: passes to recruiter immediately. Anomaly detected: Layer 2 triggered.
Layer 2 — Challenge
Targeted async task sent. Behavioral biometrics scored during response. Baseline match: candidate cleared. Behavioral divergence confirmed: Layer 3 triggered.
Layer 3 — Human review
Sentriq analyst reviews full signal stack + cross-company graph. Recommendation prepared for recruiter. Employer makes final decision.
Output
Recruiter sees a clean, reviewed queue. Flagged cases come with analyst context. No ATS workflow change. Full audit trail for regulatory defense.
