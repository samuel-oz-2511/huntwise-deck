What Sentriq does

Sentriq detects distortion in the hiring pipeline before it reaches a recruiter. Not identity verification — behavioral authentication. We do not ask whether a credential exists. We detect whether the person behaving in front of us matches the person who built that profile.

Attack-first foundation: Built on top of Huntwise — a live ATS exploitation tool with 100 beta users. 17 documented attack techniques the vendors didn't know existed. We know how the fraud works because we built it. No competitor has this foundation.
ATS-agnostic: Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, iCIMS, 50+ platforms. No change to employer workflow.

CAF — how it processes a candidate
Layer 1

Passive baseline captured. Score calculated. Clean: passes to recruiter. Anomaly: Layer 2 triggered.

Layer 2

Async challenge sent. Behavioral biometrics scored. Match: cleared. Divergence: Layer 3 triggered.

Layer 3

Analyst reviews full signal stack + cross-company graph. Recommendation to recruiter.

Output

Recruiter sees clean queue + flagged cases with context. Full audit trail. Employer decides.
