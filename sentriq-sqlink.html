How Sentriq Works
Layer 1

Behavioral Baseline

Passive, invisible. Captures how the application was completed: timing, navigation, input patterns. No candidate friction.

  • SDK embedded in application form
  • Works across any ATS or custom intake
  • Zero impact on candidate experience
Layer 2

Async Clarification

Triggered only when Layer 1 finds a gap worth surfacing. A short, specific question tied to what was detected. Candidates with strong applications never see it.

  • Not a quiz, not a test, not a CAPTCHA
  • Candidates answer in their own words
  • Adds signal, never removes candidates
Layer 3

Human Review

When Layer 2 flags divergence, a Sentriq analyst reviews the full signal stack and surfaces a trust signal alongside the application. The recruiter decides.

  • Zero automated rejections at any stage
  • Every flag includes a plain-language reason
  • EU AI Act compliant by design
