The Problem

You cannot tell who is real
anymore. Nobody can.

The everyday version
AI tools generate perfect CVs, pass keyword screening, feed live video answers. Recruiters process more applications than ever and trust fewer of them.
The extreme version
North Korea places 100,000+ workers inside Western tech companies using the same techniques. KnowBe4 hired one in 2024. Four video interviews, full background check. Caught day one installing malware.
The method is the same. The stakes differ. The fix is identical.

What We Built

We built the attack first.
Now we build the defense.

Huntwise is a live tool that exploits ATS systems the way adversaries do. 17 techniques the vendors didn't know existed. Sentriq is the defense built directly from that research.

1
Application Integrity
Passive behavioral signals at submission — keystroke dynamics, copy-paste, session timing. No candidate friction.
2
Async Challenge
Anomaly detected: candidate completes a short task calibrated to their claimed profile. Scoring combines behavioral signals and content quality. A proxy cannot replicate both the fingerprint and the demonstrated knowledge of the person they are impersonating.
3
Human Review
Flagged cases reach an analyst, not a recruiter. Hiring team always decides. This is also the EU AI Act Annex III compliance answer.

The Network Effect

Each new company makes it
stronger for all of them.

The three layers are the product. The cross-company graph is the moat. ATS vendors are contractually barred from cross-employer data sharing. Sentriq is a neutral third party — candidates consent to us directly. No one inside the hiring stack can build this.

Customer 100

Why it cannot be copied
Requires neutral third-party status + direct candidate consent + behavioral detection running at scale. Simultaneously. No existing player has all three.
