The Product

CAF: Candidate Application Firewall.

The integrity layer between candidates and your hiring team. Every application passes through three signal layers before a human ever sees it.

🚫
Layer 1: Auto-Flag

Application integrity engine detects synthetic resumes, identity inconsistencies, device fingerprint anomalies, and bot-pattern submissions. Suspicious applications route to review. Not removed. Reviewed.

⏱
Layer 2: Async Challenge

A 2-minute task sent directly to the candidate. Captures keystroke dynamics, time-to-first-response, copy-paste behavior, and revision patterns. No live session required. No scheduling friction.

👁
Layer 3: Human Review

Recruiter sees an evidence card: what was flagged, why, and what the behavioral signal shows. They decide. Zero auto-rejections. Ever. Every flag is a human decision. Fully defensible under EU AI Act.

Before submission. Like Cloudflare for web traffic, Sentriq intercepts at data entry — before the application reaches any system. ATS, CRM, Google Doc: the intake tool is irrelevant.
Cross-company fraud graph. A fraudster flagged at one employer is flagged at all. Structurally unavailable to any ATS vendor.
Origin advantage. Both founders built Huntwise: the tool that breaks hiring signal. Detection signatures came from 30+ live ATS experiments on the attack side.
