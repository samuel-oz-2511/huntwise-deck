Likely Objections + Responses
"You still have no paying customers."
Correct, and we're being transparent about that. What we have: 17 documented attack techniques no competitor has, a live working demo, and active enterprise conversations that close within 14 days. We raised $300K of our own capital to earn this knowledge. The seed pays for production and SOC 2, which is what unlocks the first enterprise close.
"Persona, Checkr, Greenhouse are already in this space."
They solve identity, not behavioral fraud. DPRK operatives use real stolen SSNs. They pass Persona. They passed four video interviews and a full background check at KnowBe4. Persona confirms the document is real. We detect that the behavior doesn't match the claimed profile. Different layer entirely. And Greenhouse contracts legally prohibit cross-employer data sharing. They cannot build the cross-company graph.
"Why can't an ATS just build this?"
Greenhouse controls ~30% of the market. Even if they shipped a fraud feature tomorrow, they only see 30% of candidate flow. A fraudster applying across Greenhouse, Comeet, Workday, and a Google Form: Greenhouse sees one application. Sentriq sees all four. And their contracts prohibit cross-employer data sharing. It's a legal constraint, not a roadmap gap.
"The market for this is HR, which has small budgets."
The entry point is HR. The budget owner is CISO. After the DPRK headlines, fake employee equals security incident. CISO now owns who gets inside. CISO budget is 10-50x HR budget. EU AI Act creates a forced compliance purchase event in Q3 2026. The pricing conversation changes entirely when the CISO is in the room.
"Why did the raise go from $4M to $5M?"
We modeled the full enterprise sales cycle after M1. SOC 2, CISO buyer, 18-month runway to $1M ARR. The extra $1M is specifically engineering and compliance headcount needed before the first enterprise close. Same milestones. Same timeline. More room to execute without cutting corners on compliance.
"The network effect only works at scale. You're pre-product."
The product works from day one through absolute anomaly detection. No prior data needed. The network effect is the accelerant, not the foundation. At 15 customers, patterns emerge that no single company sees alone. That's the moat that forms as we grow. And ATS vendors can never build it due to contractual constraints.
