What Sentriq Does

A trust layer upstream of the ATS, invisible to legitimate applicants

Sentriq integrates as a webhook on the careers page. Every application passes through three layers before it reaches the recruiter. The product is live: demo available on any call.

01
Behavioral Baseline

Passive monitoring of how the form was filled: automation patterns, submission timing, behavioral fingerprinting. No friction. Platform-agnostic. Upstream of every ATS.

02
Async Challenge

A personalized micro-challenge served only when anomalies are detected. Invisible to legitimate candidates. A wall for automated and fabricated submissions. Role- and profile-specific.
