What Sentriq Does

A trust layer that sits before the ATS, invisible to the applicant

Sentriq integrates as a webhook on the careers page. Every application that enters the funnel passes through three layers before it reaches the recruiter.

01
Behavioral Detection

Monitors how the application was filled: bot vs. human, automation patterns, submission timing, behavioral anomalies. Platform-agnostic -- works upstream of every ATS.

02
Async Challenge

When an anomaly is detected, a personalized micro-challenge is served -- invisible to legitimate candidates, a wall for automated submissions. Fully customized to the role and the candidate's stated profile.

03
Human Review + ATS Enrichment

An analyst reviews flagged applications and delivers a trust signal directly inside the recruiter's existing ATS. No new dashboard. EU AI Act compliant audit trail on every decision.
