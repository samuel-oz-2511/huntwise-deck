How Sentriq Works

Three layers. Pre-submission. Platform-agnostic.

Think of Sentriq as Cloudflare for the hiring pipeline. Cloudflare sits between the internet and your web infrastructure, invisible to legitimate traffic, lethal to automated attacks. Sentriq sits between candidates and your ATS. Real applicants pass through without friction. Synthetic applications do not.

Layer 1

Behavioral Baseline

Every application carries a behavioral fingerprint: timing patterns, session depth, form interaction, device signals. A human filling out a job application behaves differently from a script running at scale. Sentriq reads that signal before the application reaches the ATS.

Layer 2

Async Challenge

Cross-referenced against known threat patterns and cross-company signals. The same submission behavior flagged at one employer surfaces as a trust signal at another. A fraudulent actor cannot reset their behavioral fingerprint across the network. The graph is the moat.

Layer 3

Human Review

For applications that cross a risk threshold, Sentriq serves a personalized async challenge that a script cannot complete. Not a generic CAPTCHA. A task calibrated to the role and the behavioral signal. Humans pass. Automated submissions do not.

Platform-agnostic. Operates as a pre-submission layer, upstream of any ATS. No rip-and-replace. No change to recruiter workflow. Trust signals surface inside the existing recruiter interface. EU AI Act Article 9 compliant by design.
The Moat

A cross-company application graph that gets stronger with every employer.

Cross-Company Signal
