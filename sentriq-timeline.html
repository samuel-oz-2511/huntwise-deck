The 13.3% ESOP pool is the authorized maximum. Unvested options at exit return to the common pool and flow back to founders and common shareholders pro-rata. Vested grants at exit — typically held by the 10-person team at Series A and all subsequent hires — vest fully on a change of control (standard double-trigger acceleration). This pool is the mechanism for retaining the engineers, ML team, and VP Sales who build the company between seed and exit.

What Moves You Between Scenarios
Three variables account for most of the range between conservative and optimistic
⚡
ATS Native Integration
Workday alone has 11,500 customers. If Sentriq becomes a native integration, distribution compounds without proportional CAC. Without it: 30-40 new logos per year. With it: thousands of activations per quarter.
Conservative to Optimistic shift
🔒
One High-Profile Breach
A publicly named enterprise breach traced to a fraudulent offshore hire creates a forcing function no sales motion can replicate. The DPRK advisory drove inbound across every hiring security company in 2024. One named victim shifts the market.
Conservative to Base shift
📋
Regulatory Mandate
CMMC 2.0 already requires workforce integrity verification for DoD supply chain. If that extends to cleared roles broadly, or if the EU AI Act creates a hiring verification obligation, government and EU become mandatory buyers.
Accelerates every stage by 6-12 months
