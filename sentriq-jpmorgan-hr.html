How Sentriq Works

Think of Sentriq as a trust layer between candidates and your ATS. Legitimate applicants pass through without friction. Fabricated and low-intent applications are flagged before any recruiter time is spent. No change to your workflow. The signal surfaces inside your existing recruiter interface.

1

Behavioral Baseline

Every application carries a behavioral fingerprint: timing patterns, session depth, form interaction, device signals. A human filling out a job application behaves differently from a script running at scale. Sentriq reads that signal before the application reaches your ATS.

2

Async Challenge

When Layer 1 flags an anomaly, a short async task is served as part of the application. Not a CAPTCHA. A role-calibrated task that detects proxy impersonation and AI-assisted submissions. Real applicants complete it without friction. Manufactured applications do not.

3

Human Review

When Layer 2 flags divergence, a Sentriq analyst reviews the full signal stack. The employer decides. Zero automated rejections. Every decision is documented for EU AI Act audit trail compliance.

Sentriq is platform-agnostic. It operates as a pre-submission layer, upstream of any ATS. No rip-and-replace. No change to recruiter workflow. Trust signals surface inside your existing recruiter interface.
