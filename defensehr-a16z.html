Platform-agnostic - works above any ATS, careers page, or application entry point. Not a replacement. A layer on top.

Layer 1: Auto-Flag

Every application analyzed for integrity signals: fabricated credentials, timeline manipulation, skill inflation, behavioral anomalies. Suspicious applications routed to a review queue. Nothing removed silently.

Layer 2: Async Challenge

Flagged candidates receive a 2-minute verification task targeting the specific gaps detected. Results feed back into the integrity signal. Honest candidates clear. Fabricated profiles collapse.

Layer 3: Human Review

Recruiters see evidence cards, not scores. Every flag is backed by specific evidence. A human always makes the final call. Zero automated rejections. Ever. Human-in-the-loop is core architecture.
