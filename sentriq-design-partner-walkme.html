The Product

Sentriq: Candidate Hiring Captcha.

Three signal layers between every candidate and your hiring team. Triggered only where there's evidence of risk. Invisible to clean candidates.

🚫
Layer 1 , Application Integrity

Captures behavioral baseline as the candidate fills the form: typing dynamics, mouse movement, fill patterns. Detects bots, mass-apply fingerprints, and skills anomalies. Invisible to clean candidates.

⏱
Layer 2 , Async Challenge

Triggered on anomaly only. A 2-minute async task. Captures keystroke dynamics, copy-paste behavior, revision patterns. Friction by design, only for candidates who triggered Layer 1.

👁
Layer 3 , Human Review

Analyst reviews the evidence card before any recruiter sees the application. Every flag is a human decision. Zero auto-rejections, ever. Fully defensible under EU AI Act Article 22.
