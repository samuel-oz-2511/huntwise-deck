The Product

CAF: Candidate Application Firewall.

Three signal layers between every candidate and your hiring team. Triggered only where there's evidence of risk. Invisible to clean candidates.

🚫
Layer 1 — Application Integrity

Passive. Captures behavioral baseline as the candidate fills the form — typing dynamics, mouse movement, fill patterns. Detects bots, mass-apply fingerprints, and CV-to-JD overfitting before a human ever sees the application. No friction for clean candidates. Invisible.

⏱
Layer 2 — Async Challenge

Triggered only on anomaly. A 2 to 3 minute task sent to the candidate. Captures keystroke dynamics, time-to-first-response, copy-paste behavior, and revision patterns. No live session required. Friction by design — only for candidates who triggered Layer 1.

👁
Layer 3 — Human Review

Analyst reviews the evidence card before a recruiter ever sees the application. What was flagged, why, and what the behavioral signal shows. Every flag is a human decision. Zero auto-rejections — ever. Fully defensible under EU AI Act Article 22.
