Defensibility

Four structural advantages. All time-gated.

01

Behavioral biometric baselines

The async challenge captures keystroke dynamics, time-to-first-response, copy-paste behavior, and revision patterns. After thousands of real sessions: baseline distributions no one else has. What a real senior engineer looks like answering under pressure vs. ChatGPT. Cannot be purchased. Cannot be shortcut. A competitor starting today starts with zero baselines.

02

Cross-company candidate graph

Device fingerprints, resume content hashing, IP range patterns, challenge fingerprints, bulk-apply timing. The graph accumulates with every customer. ATS incumbents can never build this (contractually prohibited from sharing cross-customer data). We can. We are the only independent layer that can collect direct candidate consent for cross-company matching.

03

Regulatory audit trail as switching cost

Every hiring decision through Sentriq is logged, timestamped, tied to a human review action. For regulated industries under EU AI Act, that history has legal value. Switching to a competitor means starting the compliance record from zero. Switching costs compound from day one of the first enterprise customer.

04

Challenge calibration library

After 10,000 real sessions: false-positive rates by role, seniority, and geography. Answer patterns correlated with confirmed fraud. A competitor launching today runs their first challenge with zero calibration. High false-positive rate. Enterprise customers complain. Trust breaks. We have been calibrating since customer one.

All four require real usage to build. Cannot be shortcut with capital alone. Each new customer accelerates all four simultaneously.
