ATS contracts legally prohibit cross-client candidate data sharing. Sentriq obtains direct candidate consent as a neutral third party. That consent mechanism is permanently unavailable to any ATS.

This gap is permanent. Not a head start. Structural.

WordPress ran ~30% of all websites. Rate-limiting and bot plugins existed inside WordPress. CloudFlare still dominated and still does. Companies used both. The independent trust layer won because it was cross-platform.

Greenhouse = the platform. Sentriq = the trust layer on top, regardless of which platform.
