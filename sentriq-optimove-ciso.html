Why This Is Urgent for Optimove

You hold your clients' most sensitive behavioral data. A fraudulent hire is a third-party breach.

Your data posture

Optimove processes behavioral, transactional, and demographic PII on behalf of enterprise clients in gaming, financial services, and retail. A new hire in engineering, data, or customer success gets access to client data environments within weeks. An operative does not need to breach your perimeter. They apply for a job.

Your hiring moment

Optimove raised $75M in February 2024 and is actively expanding US operations from NYC. High hiring velocity in a new geography is exactly when the attack surface peaks. Easy Apply volumes from US candidates are impossible to manually audit at scale.

Your client exposure

Your 77 enterprise clients include gaming platforms and financial services companies. If a fraudulent hire exfiltrates data from one of those environments, the breach is theirs to disclose to regulators, but the origin is your onboarding process. Your SOC 2 Type II controls do not currently extend to who you hire.

Your compliance roadmap

EU AI Act Article 9 classifies hiring AI as high-risk, with a compliance deadline of August 2026. Your ISO 27001 certification already documents your data access controls. The logical extension is audit-ready documentation of who was hired and how their application was evaluated.

The question is not whether a sophisticated attacker could target Optimove through its hiring funnel. The question is whether you would know if they already tried.

The Structural Gap

Every security control you have stops at the front door. The hiring funnel is the door.

State-backed operatives clear Okta. They pass Persona. They survive background checks with real stolen SSNs. Every layer below protects data once someone is inside. None of them determine whether the person who just joined should have been hired.

The Product

Three layers. Zero friction on real candidates. Zero automated rejections.

Platform-agnostic. Works upstream of Greenhouse. Invisible to legitimate applicants. Every rejection decision stays with your team.

Layer 1

Behavioral Baseline

Passive, invisible. Captures behavioral metadata while the application form is being filled: timing, rhythm, device signals, input patterns. No friction. No candidate notification. Triggers Layer 2 only on anomaly.

Layer 2

Async Challenge

Framed as part of the application process. Detects proxy impersonation and AI-assisted completion. Fires only when Layer 1 flags an anomaly. Invisible to clean applicants who never triggered the baseline.

Layer 3

Human Review

Sentriq analyst reviews the full signal stack and surfaces a trust signal to your team. The hiring decision remains entirely with Optimove. Zero automated rejections. EU AI Act compliant by architecture.

The network effect

Every verified application adds to a cross-company behavioral graph. A signal pattern flagged at one company becomes a pre-trained baseline at others. Sentriq gets stronger with every customer added. No ATS vendor or background check provider can build this from inside a single company's data.

Integration

Upstream of Greenhouse. Zero workflow disruption for your team.

Sentriq does not replace your ATS, your interview process, or your hiring decisions. It adds a behavioral verification layer before the application reaches your recruiter's queue.

Compliance Alignment

Built for companies that already take compliance seriously. Three frameworks. One deployment.

SOC 2 Type II

Sentriq generates a per-application audit trail: signal data, timestamps, reviewer identity, and disposition. This extends your existing access control documentation upstream to the point of hire, not just the point of onboarding.

EU AI Act

Article 9 classifies hiring AI as high-risk. Compliance deadline: August 2026. Sentriq is compliant by architecture: no automated rejections, human in the loop for every flagged application, full decision documentation. Optimove's GDPR posture makes this a natural extension.

GDPR / Data Processing

Sentriq processes behavioral metadata, not biometric data. The unit of analysis is the application submission event, not the candidate's identity. Data processing agreements are available. No biometric classification under GDPR Article 9.

Optimove completed SOC 2 Type II in 2022 and holds ISO 27001. You have already built the compliance culture. Sentriq extends it to the one attack surface your current certification does not cover.

The August 2026 EU AI Act deadline is 14 months away. Enterprise compliance programs that start now have a clean implementation. Those that start after the deadline have a remediation project.
