Integrates with Greenhouse, Workday, Lever, Comeet, iCIMS, and 50+ ATS platforms. Not a replacement. A layer on top.

Layer 1: Auto-Flag

Every application analyzed for integrity signals: fabricated credentials, timeline manipulation, skill inflation, behavioral anomalies. Suspicious applications routed to review queue. Nothing removed silently.

Layer 2: Async Challenge

Flagged candidates receive a 2-minute verification task targeting the specific gaps detected. Results feed back into the integrity signal. Honest candidates clear. Fabricated profiles collapse.

Layer 3: Human Review

Recruiters see evidence cards backed by specific findings. A human always makes the final call. Zero automated rejections. Ever. Human-in-the-loop is core architecture, not a disclaimer.
