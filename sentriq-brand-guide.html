Company

Category
Hiring Integrity Infrastructure
One-liner
"The integrity layer between every candidate and every hiring decision."
The Cloudflare analogy
Pre-submission layer. Upstream of everything. Platform-agnostic. The application never reaches the ATS without passing through Sentriq first.
Buyers
Head of Talent (champion). CISO (economic buyer).
Origin
Samuel Kemper and Ben Czarninski built Huntwise: a tool to help candidates beat ATS systems. 73,500 lines of code. 30+ ATS experiments. Then they flipped it. They built the offense, so they built the defense. Sentriq is that defense.

02 / The Mark

Pending
Candidate submitted. Sentriq has not yet processed the signals.
Scanning
Signal arcs pulse. Sentriq is processing behavioral and identity layers.
Cleared
The bracket resolves to the V. The same form. The arms were always the V.
The V is latent in the bracket. The two bracket arms are the two arms of the V. When a candidate clears, the mark does not switch to a new symbol. The same form resolves. There is no product reveal, no celebration icon. The bracket was always a gate. The V is what you see when the gate is passed.
