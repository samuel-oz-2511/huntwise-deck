Defensibility

Four structural advantages. Each compounds with usage.

01

Behavioral biometrics from the challenge

The async challenge captures keystroke dynamics, time-to-first-response, copy-paste patterns, and revision behavior. After thousands of real sessions we build baseline distributions no one else has — what a genuine expert looks like answering under pressure vs. someone using ChatGPT. This calibration is time-gated and cannot be purchased. A competitor starting today starts with zero baselines.

02

Cross-company graph — legally unavailable to ATS

Hiring fraud is cross-industry, not ATS-specific. An ATS by definition serves one employer at a time. Sentriq sits between the candidate and the human regardless of system: paper, Excel, Greenhouse, or in-house builds. ATS incumbents also cannot share cross-company candidate data contractually — their customer agreements legally prohibit it. Sentriq gets direct candidate consent as an independent integrity layer. That consent mechanism is structurally unavailable to any ATS, ever. At 15 companies, our graph detects patterns no single company sees alone.

03

Regulatory audit trail as switching cost

Every hiring decision through Sentriq is logged, timestamped, and tied to a human review action. For regulated industries — financial services, healthcare, defense contractors — that audit history has legal value under EU AI Act. Switching to a competitor means starting the compliance record from zero. Switching costs compound from day one of the first enterprise customer.

04

Challenge calibration library

After 10,000 real challenge sessions, we know false-positive rates by role, seniority, and geography. We know which answer patterns correlate with confirmed fraud. A competitor launching today runs their first challenge with zero calibration — high false-positive rate, enterprise customers complain, trust breaks. We have been calibrating since customer one.

All four advantages are time-gated — they require real usage to build and cannot be shortcut with capital alone. Each new customer accelerates all four simultaneously.
