Infrastructure
No icon. Wide tracking, heavy weight, amber underline. Reads as a system identifier — a protocol name, a layer designation. Scales from email footer to slide header without adaptation. Dark and light both work without changing a single character. The underline is the only color element, which keeps attention on the name.

Design 04
Binary Split
SENTR in light weight, IQ in bold amber. The name breaks exactly where the meaning shifts: protection into intelligence. No separator, no decoration — the contrast does the work. Every time someone reads the name, the duality is visible in the mark itself. Collapses cleanly to monochrome if needed.

Design 05
Q-Mark
The Q is both a letter and an icon. In the wordmark, SENTRI carries in white, then the Q breaks to amber — heavier, warmer, branded. Used standalone, the amber Q-square is your app icon, favicon, email stamp, and slide watermark. No additional icon asset needed. Over time, the Q-mark becomes recognizable without the full name next to it — the same path Cloudflare's flame and Stripe's "S" traveled. One letter. One shape. One system.
